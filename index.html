Urban Sleek City Haven BNB: Features

Situated in Cape Town, Ubarn Sleek City Haven BnB features accommodation with private pool, free WiFi and free private parking for guests who drive. With pool views, this accommodation offers a patio. The property is non-smoking and is set 2.8km from Milnerton Beach.

This 1-bedroom aparment comes with a fully equipped kitchen, a seating area, and a flat-screen Smart TV. The property offers garden views.

Guests can relax in the garden at the property.

CTICC is 11km from the apartment, while Robben Island Ferry is 12 km from the property. The nearest airport is Cape Town Internation Airport, 17km from Ubarn Sleek City Haven BnB.
